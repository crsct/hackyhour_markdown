tags:
- desserts

ingredients:
- 500 ml milk
- 200 ml cream
- 3 tbps sugar
- 40 g starch
- 1 tsp cardamon
- 1 tsp orangeblossom water
- some almond shards

directions:
- Combine milk, cream and simmer
- let it boil while stirring adding a starch slurry
- add orange or rosewater
- let it cool
- garnish with almond shards

---

Muhalabia is a delicate, silky milk pudding with roots across the Middle East and North Africa, including Morocco. Lightly sweetened and subtly perfumed with **orange blossom water** or rose water, it has a soft, creamy texture that melts on the tongue. Traditionally set with cornstarch or rice flour, it's chilled until just firm and served garnished with crushed **pistachios**, toasted almonds, or a drizzle of honey — sometimes with a dusting of cinnamon. It's a beautifully simple dessert that feels both refreshing and indulgent, often enjoyed after a rich Moroccan meal.
